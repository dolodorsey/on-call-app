FAQ

How do I book a service?
Open the app, select a service category, choose your preferred time, and confirm your booking.

How do I become a service provider?
Open the app and tap "Provider Portal" to create a Hero account.

Is my payment information secure?
Yes. All payments are processed through industry-standard encrypted channels.

Can I cancel a booking?
Yes. You can cancel up to 30 minutes before your scheduled service with no fee.
